What Is EVOSGPT?

EVOSGPT is an evolving AI built for students, developers, creators, and everyday users who want fast, secure, intelligent assistance. It adapts to your workflow, helps you think, build, write, study, explore, create, and improve — all while keeping your privacy protected.

How to Use EVOSGPT

  • Ask anything — homework, coding, ideas, summaries, explanations.
  • Use commands for better results:
    /explain – break down concepts
    /rewrite – improve text
    /summarize – shorten content
    /build – generate structured output
    /fix – debug or correct text/code
  • Pin chats to keep important conversations (coming soon).
  • Upgrade tiers anytime for more speed, depth, and capability.
  • Everything is private — no tracking, no external logging.

Tier Overview

EVOSGPT tiers are designed for different types of users:

  • Basic — Quick answers, light usage, great for students.
  • Core — More power, longer messages, ideal for study + coding.
  • Pro — For developers, creators, researchers, and heavy workflow.
  • King — Maximum limits, highest speed, premium capabilities.

Final pricing appears only after choosing a tier.
